About Our Food

We’re Passionate About Our Food

From adding more balanced options to our Happy Meal®, to serving up fresh beef Quarter Pounder® burgers that are cooked when you order, we’re always finding ways to show our commitment to our customers and our food. We’re passionate about our food. That’s why we’re committed to always evolving what matters to you. From our chefs, to our dieticians and suppliers, McDuck’s food experts care deeply about the food you eat. Our Quarter Pounder® patty is made with 100% fresh beef and cooked right when you order. It’s hot and deliciously juicy and full of flavor. When your kids enjoy a meal that’s delicious and balanced, you’ll enjoy it too. When it comes to quality options, we’ve got you covered.

Commitment to Quality

We're dedicated to improving the way we prepare our quality food and the ingredients that go into it.
